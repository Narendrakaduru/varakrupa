About

Passion for design started as a child, as the entire family is into the business of Jewellery from three generations. Varakrupa designs 22 carat pure gold design and mainly focused and specialised into hand crafted antique jewellery for anyone who's passed through fire to wear as an emblem of triumph. Our work is a tribute to our heritage, to perseverance and to the spirit of triumph in you.
