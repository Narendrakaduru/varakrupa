A Few Words About Us

Passion for design started as a child, as the entire family is into the business of Jewellery from three generations.

Varakrupa designs 22 carat pure gold design and mainly focused and specialised into hand crafted antique jewellery for anyone who's passed through fire to wear as an emblem of triumph. Our work is a tribute to our heritage, to perseverance and to the spirit of triumph in you.

We continually develop our jewellery portfolio in keeping with our core values of affordable luxury, contemporary design and personal storytelling.

We are the manufacturers and wholesalers where we focus more on B2B markets to satisfy the customer needs. We have ample customers across the globe.
